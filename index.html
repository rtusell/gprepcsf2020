Computer Science Fundamentals

Summer 2020

Welcome!

Welcome to our website for our Computer Science Fundamentals class for summer of 2020. The students were asked to program two different games as their final project for the session. For their first game everyone had to code a game of breakout. For their second game students had a choice to program either: snake, tic-tac-toe or a helicopter game. Feel free to browse our webpage and play with some of the games created by our students. Thank you to the Prep community for the support during the summer and to CodeHS and w3schools for suppling the curriculum and HTML, CSS and JavaScript documentation.
